Introduction

Cocktail Galore (company, organization, etc.)

Cocktail Galore is a cocktail bar focused on making unique and delciious drinks!

Young adults and working professionals are our target market.

Customers will use this site in order to check the hours, check which cocktails are available, and look into what type of bar it is.

The primary task of the Cocktail Galore is to create a space where people are able to chill and hang out with friends while trying out new cocktails!

About

Cocktail Galore is a central coast originated cocktail lounge. Our goal is to have a space where customers can spend after work or during the weekend. Customers will be able to try new cocktails, spend time with friends, and enjoy a nice ambience and environment.
